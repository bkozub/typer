How to type - Guide

  • Login or register in Typer application.
  • Click 'Type' on the top bar of web page, select place, competition and sky jumper.
  • You can choose only first five place of competition.
  • If you wanna see your types, click 'Account' on the right corner of screen and select correct option from dropdown list.
  • Typing is closed 5 minutes before the start of the competition.
  • Typing is closed 5 minutes before the start of the competition.
  • The rules for awarding points:
    • Proper selection of a player from the top five jumping competition, who took exactly the place that the user indicated -5 points;
    • Predict the top five jumping contestants in the top five but in a different position than the one indicated by the user -2 points;
    • Correct indication of the winner of the jumping contest -additional 3 points;
  • The results will be given within 1 day after the competition.
